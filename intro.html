Welcome to my web development notes#

These are the notes I took while attending the freeCodeCamp course on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They’re intended to help me study and remember what I’ve learnt so far. If you’ve found this by some strange coincidence, please note that I’m not the creator of the course, nor am I pretending to be.

This site was made possible thanks to Jupyter Book and GitHub Pages.
